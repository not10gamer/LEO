The Beginning

The LEO Club of Ethos International College was founded in 2022 with the goal of empowering young leaders who strive to serve their community and grow through meaningful experiences. The founding members, led by our first president Rachel Desilva, shared a common passion for making a difference through action, leadership, and integrity. Their vision continues to guide the club’s activities, shaping its future endeavors.

Guided by Core Values

At the heart of the LEO Club of Ethos lies a commitment to serve the community while developing leadership skills. The core values that shaped our club’s foundation include leadership, service, and community engagement. These values are not only reflected in our activities but also in the growth of each member who contributes to the vision of a better tomorrow.

Key Milestones and Achievements

Throughout our journey, we have celebrated several key milestones, including our first major community project, the first fundraising event, and recognition for outstanding service. These achievements mark the progress of our club’s mission to make a lasting impact on the community and foster future leaders who will continue this legacy.

The Vision of Our Founders

The vision of our founders, led by Rachel Desilva, was to create a space where youth could grow through service and leadership. Their passion for social change has been a source of inspiration for current and future members, driving the LEO Club forward in its mission to make a difference.
