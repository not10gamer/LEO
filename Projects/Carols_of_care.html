Carols of care

Organized by the Leo Club of Ethos International College, aimed to spread joy and warmth to the children at Lady Ridgeway Hospital.

A post shared by Leo Club of Ethos International College (@leoclubofeic)

Project Information

The objective of the "Carols of Care" project was to enhance the well-being and comfort of children undergoing treatment at Lady Ridgeway Hospital while spreading joy and festive cheer. This was achieved by addressing the physical need for improved facilities through the donation of an air conditioner and meeting emotional needs by performing Christmas carols to uplift their spirits. The project aimed to create a positive and compassionate environment, reinforcing the Leo Club's commitment to serving the community and bringing meaningful change to vulnerable groups.

The "Carols of Care" initiative by the Leo Club of Ethos International College significantly benefited the community by enhancing the comfort of children at Lady Ridgeway Hospital through donations and festive activities. The project uplifted the spirits of young patients and their families, offering emotional relief during challenging times, while fostering a sense of joy and positivity within the hospital.
