F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can join the LEO of EIC?

    Anyone student of Ethos International College.

  • What activities does the LEO of EIC organize?

    The LEO Club of EIC organizes community service, workshops, fundraising events, and social activities to empower youth and support the community.

  • How can joining LEO benefit me?

    Joining the LEO Club enhances your leadership skills, provides community service experience, expands your network, and fosters personal growth while making a positive impact.

  • Are there leadership opportunities within LEO?

    Yes, LEO Club offers numerous leadership opportunities, including roles in club management, project coordination, event planning, and mentorship positions, allowing members to develop and practice their leadership skills.
