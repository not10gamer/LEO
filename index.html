What is LEO?

The LEO Club is a youth organization affiliated with Lions Clubs International.

It focuses on developing leadership skills, promoting community service, and fostering personal growth among young members. Through various projects and activities, members gain experience in teamwork, responsibility, and service, all while contributing to their local communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Our Answers: Everything You Need to Know!

1. How can joining LEO benefit me?

Joining the LEO Club enhances your leadership skills, provides community service experience, expands your network, and fosters personal growth while making a positive impact.

2. Are there leadership opportunities within LEO?

Yes, LEO Club offers numerous leadership opportunities, including roles in club management, project coordination, event planning, and mentorship positions, allowing members to develop and practice their leadership skills.

3. What activities does the LEO of EIC organize?

The LEO Club of EIC organizes community service, workshops, fundraising events, and social activities to empower youth and support the community.
